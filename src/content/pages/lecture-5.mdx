A prediction is only valuable if someone acts on it. A churn score of 0.87 means nothing to a marketing manager who needs to decide what to do about it. This lecture wraps the prediction API with an LLM — turning raw scores into natural-language explanations, suggested actions, and interactive Q&A.

Students build the *hybrid architecture* that defines modern AI products: a prediction engine with an intelligent conversational front-end. We face the hard problems head-on: hallucination risk, prompt injection, and LLM safety guardrails. This is the lecture that makes this course different from every other MLOps course.

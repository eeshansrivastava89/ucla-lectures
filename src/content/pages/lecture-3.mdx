"It works on my machine" is not a deployment strategy. Containers solve the environment problem: same code, same dependencies, same behavior everywhere. This lecture wraps the prediction service in a container and adds orchestration so the system retrains on a schedule instead of waiting for someone to remember.

Students go from a brittle laptop setup to something reproducible. Docker guarantees the environment. Prefect guarantees the process. Together they turn a script into a system that runs the same way every time, whether it is Tuesday at noon or Sunday at 3am.

Every ML project starts in a notebook. But a notebook is not a product — it's a prototype. In this lecture, we take a trained model and deploy it as a live prediction API that anyone can call.

Students leave with a working endpoint and the mental model: *if it's not deployed, it doesn't exist.* This is the single most important shift from academic ML to production ML — and most courses never teach it.

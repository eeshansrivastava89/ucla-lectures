A model that worked at deployment can silently fail as data shifts, pipelines break, or biases emerge. This lecture adds the *production safety layer*: tests that run automatically, monitoring that catches drift, and CI/CD that ensures every change is validated before reaching production.

We also address bias auditing — fairness is a production requirement, not a nice-to-have. A deployed model affects real people, and MSBA students will be the ones asked "is this model fair?" in their first month on the job.

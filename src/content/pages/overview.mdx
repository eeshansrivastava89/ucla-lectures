This course covers the production side of ML that most programs never teach: how to take a trained model and turn it into a deployed, monitored, explainable product. Each lecture teaches concepts through building — students start with a Jupyter notebook and end, five weeks later, with a shipped AI application.

The course is organized around a single project that grows every week: *train → deploy → track → containerize → monitor → wrap with LLM*. By the end, students have built a hybrid ML + LLM architecture — a prediction engine with an intelligent conversational front-end.

**Every concept connects back to business decisions:** what does drift mean for KPIs, why does versioning matter for audits, how does an LLM turn a score into an action?
